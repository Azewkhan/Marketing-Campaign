## 📊 Marketing Campaign Analysis: Facebook vs. AdWords

### 🔍 Project Overview

This project analyzes and compares the performance of two digital advertising platforms—Facebook Ads and Google AdWords—to identify which delivers better results in terms of conversions, engagement, and cost-effectiveness. The objective is to generate data-driven insights that help optimize budget allocation and maximize Return on Investment (ROI).

---

### 🎯 Objectives

* Perform **A/B Testing** to compare campaign performance
* Build a **Regression Model** to predict conversions from clicks
* Analyze **time-based trends** (weekly & monthly patterns)
* Evaluate **cost efficiency** using key metrics like CPC and conversion rates

---

### 📁 Dataset

The dataset contains **365 daily records (2019)** for both platforms, including:

* Clicks, Views, Conversions
* Ad Spend (Cost)
* Derived metrics: CTR, Conversion Rate, Cost per Click

---

### ⚙️ Tech Stack

* **Python** (pandas, numpy)
* **Visualization:** matplotlib, seaborn
* **Machine Learning:** scikit-learn
* **Statistical Analysis:** statsmodels

---

### 📈 Key Analysis

**1. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**

* Analyzed distributions of clicks and conversions
* Cleaned and transformed data for analysis

**2. A/B Testing (Hypothesis Testing)**

* T-test showed a **significant difference (p < 0.05)**
* Facebook outperformed AdWords in average conversions

**3. Regression Analysis**

* Built a model on Facebook data
* Achieved **R² = 0.76**, indicating strong predictive power

**4. Time Series Analysis**

* Higher engagement observed on **Mondays and Tuesdays**
* Overall upward trend with minor monthly fluctuations

**5. Co-integration Analysis**

* Identified a **long-term relationship** between ad spend and conversions

---

### 🔑 Key Findings

* **Facebook Ads outperform AdWords** in conversions and engagement
* Strong correlation between clicks and conversions on Facebook (**0.87**)
* Early weekdays are optimal for campaign launches
* Predictive modeling supports **better budget planning and ROI optimization**
